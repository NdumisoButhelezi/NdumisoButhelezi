## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 1) [POPIA-Compliant AI Inference Checker](https://github.com/NdumisoButhelezi/popia-llm-package)
A middleware system built as part of my Honours research that acts as a secure proxy for LLM interactions.  
It intercepts prompts, detects sensitive personal information, and scrubs risky data using local models before forwarding safe queries to public AI APIs.  
**Tech:** Python, Local LLMs, Agentic Workflows, Data Privacy (POPIA)  
**Impact:** Strengthens POPIA-aligned privacy controls for AI usage and reduces exposure of personally identifiable information.

### 2) [DUT Clubs & Societies Hub 🎓](https://github.com/khonCoin/dut-clubs-societies-hub) • [Live Demo](https://dut-clubs-societies-hub.netlify.app/)
A centralized student engagement platform for the Durban University of Technology, helping students discover, join, and manage clubs across Political, Academic, Social, and other categories.  
Includes secure QR-based resource access, real-time event galleries, and separate admin/member experiences.  
**Tech:** React, TypeScript, Firebase  
**Impact:** Improved discoverability and operational management for student organizations at institutional scale.
